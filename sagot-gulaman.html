Sago't Gulaman

A sweet and slushy treat with powdered milk, chocolate syrup, and colorful sprinkles.

Sago't Gulaman is a refreshing Filipino drink that combines chewy sago pearls and sweet, silky gulaman jelly in a perfectly balanced, chilled syrup. The drink is infused with a hint of caramelized sugar and vanilla, creating a smooth and satisfying sweetness with every sip. Served cold, it's the ultimate thirst-quencher that offers a delightful mix of textures and flavors, making it an irresistible treat on any hot day.
