Mango Sago

A creamy dessert featuring mangoes, sago pearls, and milk, perfect for mango lovers.

Mango Sago is a refreshing tropical dessert that combines the rich, creamy sweetness of ripe mangoes with the delightful texture of chewy sago pearls. Served chilled, this silky treat is perfect for a hot day, offering a burst of vibrant flavors in every spoonful. Topped with fresh mango chunks and a hint of coconut milk, it’s a blissful indulgence you won’t want to put down.
