Mais Con Yelo

A refreshing dessert made with sweet corn, crushed ice, and milk. Perfect for hot weather!

Mais con yelo is a refreshing Filipino dessert that combines sweet corn kernels with shaved ice, offering a cool, creamy texture. Drizzled with a generous amount of condensed milk and sometimes coconut milk, each bite delivers a perfect balance of sweetness and richness. Topped with a sprinkle of sugar and sometimes a dash of vanilla, this treat is a delightful escape from the heat, leaving you craving more with every spoonful.
